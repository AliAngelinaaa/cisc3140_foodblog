Warnings

  • Allergies: Many desserts contain common allergens such as nuts, dairy, eggs, wheat, or soy. It's important to be aware of these ingredients.

  • Cross-Contamination: If you're serving desserts in a shared setting, take precautions to prevent cross-contamination.
